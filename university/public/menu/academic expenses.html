Acedemic Expenses

 

An undergraduate student has to complete a significant number of credits (more than 150 credits depending on the department) during the 4 years program for the award of degree. Each year they have to complete 2 semester study. The allowable credits for each semester are typically in the range of 18 to 22. The table below gives an estimate of the costs and expenses a full-time undergraduate student without financial aid should expect throughout the duration of his/her study program

Tuition and registration fees are payable on semester basis. AGRONI Bank, JUST branch is used to receive the payment in the specified account number. Students are residing on campus requested to deposit payment of expenses charge at Agroni Bank in due date to avoid penalties. Students are also requested to deposit all the academic fees in due date as there is a penalties for late payment. Fees are frequently reviewed. Approved changes become effective on the date stipulated.
