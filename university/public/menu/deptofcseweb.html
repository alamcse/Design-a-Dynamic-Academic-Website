Department's View:


The aim of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering is to support students to learn basic theory and applied technology in the related fields. It also provides opportunities for students to learn the latest techniques to gain the technical skills required for the development of advanced large software products. The Computer Science and Engineering Department at JUST would like to give a commitment to hands-on study through a Lab intensive curriculum. Our commitment to a strong laboratory experience is evidenced by staffing Labs with faculty members, by closely intertwined lecture and by a large number of lab courses that are required for students.

Laboratory Facilities:
